  1. 40 Days of Dating (Tammy)
  2. Medium: Publication, Collage
  3. Paper: Domtar, "Cream"
  4. Year: 2018
  5. Format: 8 x 10 in, Inserts: 4 x 5 in
An assignment based on Jessica Walsh and Timothy Goodman's 40 Days of Dating project. Follow these rules: 1. We will see each other every day for forty days (March 13—April 20, 2018) 2. We will go on at least three off-campus 'dates' a week 3. We will go on one overnight trip together 4. We will fill out the daily questionnaire and document everything: —What were three things that 'worked'? —What did not work? —What would I do differently the next time around? —New insight about self. Our final piece documenting our new friendship combined styles of both mine and Tammy's—mine being collage and hers being poetic writing.
