  1. 100x Produce + Bags (in Target)
  2. Medium: Plush foods, Bags, Photography
  3. Paper: N/A
  4. Year: 2019
  5. Format: N/A
Besides being on everyday objects that we touch, virus pathogens lurk in the foods we eat. Ew. Yep, you got it, food poisoning. For my fellow germaphobes, or more specifically cibophobes, this a nightmare: always aware of everything we eat in fear of something being bad or infectious. Through my thesis, this fear manifested itself as products you would normally find at a supermarket: reusable bags and produce.
