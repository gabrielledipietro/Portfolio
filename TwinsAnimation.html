  1. Conjoined Twins and the Animated World
  2. Medium: Unbound Publication
  3. Paper: Matte White, 80 lb
  4. Year: 2017
  5. Format: 16 x 25 cm
Pseudo-scientific Treatise on Animation and the Perception of Movement. Scientific, empirical, unscientific ways are used to explain “phenomenons” in our world. Found images and articles are featured in the "Scientfic paper". Shapes from the Interpolated Shapes project are used as a visualization for how conjoined twins form. Spreads are removable. The viewer can only see the correct content spread pairings when removing the spread from the whole publication. Page numbers go by 1/2 to represent how conjoined twins are two people with one body.
