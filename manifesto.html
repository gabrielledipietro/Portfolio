  1. Manifesto Poster
  2. Medium: B&W Poster
  3. Paper: Engineering Print
  4. Year: 2018
  5. Format: 24 x 36 in
Poster designed around 5 randomly chosen constraints: (photography, mirror, stamp, 3D, stencil) and a rule from Bruce Mau's 'An Incomplete Manifesto for Growth'. Words were laser cut and inked as stamps. The poster is a photograph of the words sitting on top of a mirror. Rule Chosen: no. 22 "Make your own tools. Hybridize your tools in order to build unique things. Even simple tools that are your own can yield entirely new avenues of exploration. Remember, tools amplify our capacities, so even a small tool can make a big difference."
