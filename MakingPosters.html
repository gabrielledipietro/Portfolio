  1. Making Posters
  2. Medium: Poster, Cut Letters, Sticky Tack
  3. Paper: White, 80 lb
  4. Year: 2017
  5. Format: 24 x 36 in
A poster instructing one on how to make a poster for an event such as a political rally. The letters for "Making Posters" and the lines connecting the letters are removable, allowing the viewer to interact and create their own message. Influenced by a peer's Identity booklet that focused on her passion for street photography and documenting events such as city rallies. Design inspired by posters created by Team Thursday. A whole alphabet comes with kit.
