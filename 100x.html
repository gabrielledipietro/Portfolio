  1. 100x Produce
  2. Medium: Silkscreen, Fabric, Buttons
  3. Paper: Mohawk, "Linen"
  4. Year: 2019
  5. Format: N/A
I focused on a five color palette within the foods: orange, bone, yellow, red and green. I screen printed patterns on plain fabric and sewed five different types of food plushies: oranges, onions, bananas, tomatoes and limes, which totaled to over 100 plush foods. The price of the items, dollars or tickets, is left up to the viewer to decide. The virus patterns for each food are as follows: 1) Indian Citrus Ringspot: Orange, 2) Shallot Virus X: Onion, 3) Banana Streak: Banana 4) Tomato Yellow Leaf Curl: Tomato, 5) Citrus Tristeza: Lime.
