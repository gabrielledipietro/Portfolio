  1. Catalogue of Influces: Package Deal
  2. Medium: Publication, Green Thread
  3. Paper: Domtar, "Cream", AB "Martian Green"
  4. Year: 2018
  5. Format: 4.5 x 7 in, Inserts: 2 x 6 in
A book of etiquette that does not follow the rules; a "clap back" one could say. Unlike a traditional etiquette book, risque content is exposed on the cover and is hidden inside and behind green inserts ironically marked with a heart. Here articles about and quotes from 90s movies and TV featuring a strong female lead are the main content. These female characters show that being strong and independent can equate to "lady-like". This publication was sewn by myself using a sewing machine and green thread.
