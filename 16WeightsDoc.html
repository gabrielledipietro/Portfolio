  1. 16 Weights of Knockout (2/2)
  2. Medium: Publication, Photography
  3. Paper: White Glossy 80lb, "Rocket Red"
  4. Year: 2017
  5. Format: 5 x 13 in
1/2 publications that documents riding and being strapped into amusement park rides at Six Flags New England as a form of physical restraint that influences my way of drawing. The Photo Documentation book is approximately the size of a park map. This project, "Drawing Restraint", was originally written and conducted by Guy Meldem at the École cantonale d’Art de Lausanne in 2013.
