  1. Social Activism: Self-Care
  2. Medium: Sticker, Video
  3. Paper: "Hello" sticker
  4. Year: 2017
  5. Format: 43 sec video
A foam board, stickers and sharpies, were left at the St. Mary's T stop of Boston's Green Line for anyone to come up to. The board had a scale that went from "want" to "need". Participants could fill in a sticker that would describe something they either wanted or needed more of or something they wanted or needed less of in their life and place the sticker accordingly on the want to need scale.
