1/3 design responses to 99% Invisible's episode "Plaid: Articles of Interest #2". "Rights" is a response to my experience going to catholic school and how I bought my uniform at an Army Navy store. Seemingly high (private schools) and low (Army Navy stores) cultures are compared through imagrey and mission statements. The book aims to show how the places are more similar than different.
