  1. Who Owns Plaid
  2. Medium: Poster-Zine Publication
  3. Paper: Mohawk
  4. Year: 2018
  5. Format: 23 x 35 in
1/3 design responses to 99% Invisible's episode "Plaid: Articles of Interest #2". This poster-zine documents the history of plaid and how is has acted as a symbol of oppression and colonialism. Imagery and typography reflect the "patchwork/collage" feel of plaid.
