  1. 16 Weights of Knockout (1/2)
  2. Medium: Publication, Calligraphy Pen
  3. Paper: Glossy, 80lb
  4. Year: 2017
  5. Format: 5.5 x 8.5 in
1/2 publications that reflect riding and being strapped into amusement park rides at Six Flags New England as a form of physical restraint that influences my way of drawing. Drawings were done using ink and calligraphy pens. Each drawing is a weight of "Knockout," the font. The weight used for each ride was determined by a scale I fabricated that relates ride weight limits to boxing weights. This project, "Drawing Restraint," was originally written and conducted by Guy Meldem at the École cantonale d’Art de Lausanne in 2013.
