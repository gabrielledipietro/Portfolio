16 Weights of Knockout (1/2) Drawings
Medium: Publication, Calligraphy Pen
Paper: White Glossy 80lb
Year: 2017
Format: 5.5 x 8.5 in
1/2 publications that reflect riding and being strapped into amusement park rides at Six Flags New England as a form of physical restraint that influences my way of drawing. Drawings done using ink and calligraphy pens. Each drawing is a weight of "Knockout", the font. The weight used for each ride was determined by an invented scale relating ride limit weight to boxing weights. This project, "Drawing Restraint", was originally written and conducted by Guy Meldem at the École cantonale d’Art de Lausanne in 2013.
