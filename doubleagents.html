Double Agents
Medium: Poster, Acrylic, Photography
Paper: White, 120lb
Year: 2017
Format: 24 x 32 in
Poster design for 2017 MIT Architectural Lecture Series, "Double Agents". The front of the poster features laser cut acrylic letters and their respective letter shadow. Letter shadow formed by taping the letters to a wall, shining a flashlight on them/the wall in a dark room, and photographing. Back: color of months corresponds to color of event.
