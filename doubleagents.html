  1. Double Agents
  2. Medium: Poster, Acrylic, Photography
  3. Paper: White, 120lb
  4. Year: 2017
  5. Format: 24 x 32 in
Poster design for the 2017 MIT Architecture Lecture Series, Double Agents. The front of the poster features laser cut acrylic letters and their respective letter shadow. Letter shadows were formed by taping the letters to a wall, shining a flashlight on them/the wall in a dark room and photographing. Back: color of months corresponds to color of the event.
