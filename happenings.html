  1. 9 Happenings in Four Parts
  2. Medium: Publication, Brass Rings
  3. Paper: Domtar, "Gray", AB "Lunar Blue"
  4. Year: 2018
  5. Format: 7 x 10.5 in, Blue Book: 4 x 6 in
An account of my life growing up going to catholic school. There are two voices in the publication, the Catholic Church and my own. The Church is presented as the large, bubbly text while my voice is much smaller and flipped upside down. Meant to be ironic but factual. Features text from Betty Friedan's "The Feminine Mystique".
