  1. 9 Happenings in Four Parts
  2. Medium: Publication, Brass Rings
  3. Paper: Domtar, "Gray", AB "Lunar Blue"
  4. Year: 2018
  5. Format: 7 x 10.5 in, Blue: 4 x 6 in
A publication focusing on 4 artists that contributed to the "Happenings" arts movement. "A happening is a performance, event, or situation meant to be considered art, usually as performance art. The term was first used by Allan Kaprow during the 1950s to describe a range of art-related event or multiple events." Each spread is different to mimic how happenings are different for each person. There are four text sections that can be taken apart by unhooking the rings, making it possible to rearrange the content in multiple ways.
