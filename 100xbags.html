  1. 100x Reusable Bags
  2. Medium: Silkscreen, Fabric
  3. Paper: N/A
  4. Year: 2019
  5. Format: 12.25 x 13.25 x 8 in
I focused on a five color palette within the bags: orange, bone, yellow, red and green. I silkscreened four different bag designs on five different colored bags for a total of 20 unique reusable shopping bags. The images are divided by food disease: 1) Peach with Plum Pox Virus, 2) Pepper with Tomato Spotted Wilt Virus, 3) Carrots with Carrot Virus Y, and 4) Zucchini with Zucchini Yellow Mosaic Virus. The sides of each bag reads Bone Folders + Blue Gloves, the name of my design thesis show, while the bottom reads 100x Produce and gives a little information along with QR code to access the BF + BG online experience.
