  1. A–Z Thesis Dictionary
  2. Medium: Publication, Floss
  3. Paper: Mohawk, "Superfine White"
  4. Year: 2019
  5. Format: 10.5 x 7.25 in
An a–z "dictionary" of my inspirations and process as an artist and designer leading up to my thesis ideation. The publication is self-bound with a coptic stitch. I used dental floss as a replacement for bookbinding thread to reiterate my feelings towards cleanliness, hygiene and germs.
